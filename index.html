Is Maritime Pro Connect really free for homeowners? +

Yes — completely free. You will never be charged a fee to submit a project request, receive quotes, or use any feature of our platform. We generate revenue by charging contractors for leads. You pay nothing, ever.

How quickly will contractors contact me? +

Most homeowners hear from at least one contractor within a few hours. Our contractors are motivated to respond quickly — first contact usually gets the job. We aim for all leads to be responded to within 24 hours.

How do you verify your contractors? +

Every contractor is manually reviewed before being added to Maritime Pro Connect. We check business legitimacy, communication quality, service areas, and professionalism. We ask for proof of licensing and insurance as part of the application. Contractors with unresolved complaints are removed.

Up to 3 pre-screened contractors for your trade and city will reach out. This gives you enough options to compare without being overwhelmed.

Absolutely not. There is zero obligation. Getting quotes is completely free and pressure-free — you compare and decide on your own timeline.

Never. Your contact information is shared only with contractors matched to your specific request in your city. We do not sell data or share your information with any third party beyond your matched contractors.

We currently serve Greater Moncton (Moncton, Riverview, Dieppe) and Halifax Regional Municipality (Halifax, Dartmouth, Bedford). We are actively expanding to more Maritime communities.

What if I am not happy with the quotes I receive? +

Email us at maritimeproconnect@gmail.com and we will do our best to find you a better match. Our goal is to make sure every homeowner gets a quality experience.

5 Best Roofing Contractors
in Moncton, NB (2026)

How to find a licensed, insured roofer in Moncton — and what to look for before you sign anything.

Finding a good roofer in Moncton is harder than it should be. Most homeowners have no idea who's actually licensed, who carries liability insurance, and who will show up when they say they will. This guide walks you through exactly what to look for.

The 5 Things to Check Before Hiring

1. Proof of Liability Insurance

Ask for a current certificate of insurance naming you as additionally insured for the duration of the job. This protects you if a worker is injured on your property.

2. WorkSafeNB Clearance Letter

Ask for a current clearance letter from WorkSafeNB confirming they are registered and in good standing. Any legitimate contractor can pull this in 2 minutes.

3. Written Quote with Materials Specified

The quote should specify shingle brand and grade, number of layers being removed, whether ice and water shield is included, and the warranty terms.

4. Local References

Ask for 2–3 recent jobs in Greater Moncton you can call. A contractor who just finished a roof in Riverview is a safer bet than someone driving in from out of town.

5. Payment Terms — Never 100% Upfront

A reasonable structure is 10–20% deposit, 40% at material delivery, remainder on completion. Full payment upfront before work starts is a red flag.

EV Charger Installation in Halifax —
Cost & What to Expect

Everything you need to know about installing a Level 2 home EV charger in Nova Scotia.

EV ownership is growing fast in Atlantic Canada, and a home Level 2 charger is one of the best investments you can make alongside buying the car.

Level 1 (Standard Outlet)

Plugs into a regular 120V outlet. Charges at 5–8 km of range per hour. Too slow for most EVs. No installation required but takes 40+ hours for a full charge.

Level 2 (240V — Recommended)

Requires a dedicated 240V circuit. Charges at 30–50 km of range per hour. Fully charges most EVs overnight. This is what most EV owners install at home.
